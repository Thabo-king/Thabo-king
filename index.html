About Roschard Learning Institute

At Roschard Learning Institute, we believe every child is intelligent and capable of greatness. Our unique approach transforms learners from Protea Glen by addressing not only academic challenges but also building confidence and self-esteem.

Our sessions are designed to make learning enjoyable with South African flavor, producing responsible, disciplined, and confident leaders.
