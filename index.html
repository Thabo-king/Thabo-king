Welcome to Roschard Learning Institute

Located in the heart of Protea Glen, Soweto, Roschard Learning Institute is more than an extra lessons school. We are a safe space for learners from Grade 4 to 12 to grow, gain confidence, and excel academically while developing the leadership qualities that will set them up for lifelong success.

The Roschard Difference

Many intelligent students struggle with low grades, not because they lack ability, but because they lack confidence or do not find school enjoyable. At Roschard Learning Institute, we address these challenges by teaching core subjects in a way that connects to the vibrant South African spirit. Every lesson is designed to be engaging and fun, helping students see the potential in themselves.

Our Courses

We specialize in tutoring for the following subjects:

  • Mathematics
  • Accounting
  • English
  • IsiZulu
  • Geography
  • Physical Sciences

Each subject is delivered in a unique South African way that makes learning relatable and inspiring. Our passionate teachers help students see the real-world application of what they learn while building skills that go beyond the classroom.
